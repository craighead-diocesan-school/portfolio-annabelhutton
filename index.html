My Project

In our Year 10 Digital Technology class, we built games for people to play at the Year 10 Market Day. My project was ballon pop

Our game ballon pop you had to aim with a dart to pop a ballon in a square. The ballons are on some string going around in circles while our chip was picking up on noise to see weather the people would be rewarded with lollies

while we were putting the frame together we had to come up with certain shapes and sizes that woul fit our project, it had to be changed a few times along with the rasterbation we put together at first it was to big and then too small. Then we had to find a way to make the string go around the frame for the balloons to go on, it took us a few goes but ended up coming up with a better sulution. Balloons were put on and then we found they kept getting stuck around the corners so we decided to attach them with rubber bands.

We then added the micro bit to the creation to make it glow up when a loud sound happens, we sort of forgot about it on the day and it didnt really matter.

It came to the day of the market, and everything was set up and no one came...
