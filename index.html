My Project

In our Year 10 Digital Technology class, we built games for people to play at the Year 10 Market Day. My project was ballon pop

Our game ballon pop you had to aim with a dart to pop a ballon in a square. The ballons are on some string going around in circles while our chip was picking up on noise to see weather the people would be rewarded with lollies
